Online Awareness

Learn how to identify online dangers and protect yourself from cyber threats.

Phishing Scams

Be cautious of emails or messages asking for personal information. Verify before you click any links.

Fake Profiles

Always confirm who you’re connecting with online. Some fake profiles are used to steal data or spread scams.

Malware Threats

Avoid downloading unknown files or clicking suspicious pop-ups. Keep your antivirus updated.

Online Safety Tips

Practice these habits to ensure your online safety and digital well-being.

Use Strong Passwords

Combine letters, numbers, and symbols to create strong passwords. Avoid using the same one for multiple accounts.

Think Before You Share

Don’t post personal information like your address or school publicly online.

Update Regularly

Keep your apps, browsers, and software updated to stay protected from new threats.

Be Respectful

Treat others kindly online. Report any form of harassment or bullying.
